Harbour Air Mobile Application

Booking Seaplanes on the Move

 

Harbour Air Group is the world’s largest seaplane airline and they offer scheduled flights, as well as scenic tour packages in different regions across British Columbia.


The Brief:

Create a native iOS and Android application, designed from the ground up to fit how people book the seaplanes on the move.


What I Did:

Interaction / UI Design

 





In the 2017 Mobile Travel Trend survey, 90% of travel companies said that having a mobile strategy is ‘critical’ or ‘very important’ to the future success of their organization (Travelport Digital, 2018). As one of the largest all-seaplane airlines in the world, Harbour Air feels the same, with its fleet and services comparable to other top airlines.



1. Book and Manage Reservations on the Go

Users book seaplanes and check flight schedules easily on the move.



2. View Upcoming and Past Reservations

Principle prototypes to showcase the interactions.

Harbour Air Website Redesign

Browsing Seaplanes Promotions and Tours


Client: Harbour Air Ltd.

Year: 2018

My Role: Lead UX/UI Designer



The Brief:

Harbour Air Ltd. approached FreshWorks Studio for a full redesign of their website. Within the 4-month project timeline, the project team managed to redesign and develop the new site from the group up.


Solution Highlights:

  • Added users’ desired promotions and deals from research to the website content;
  • Reorganized the website’s information architecture;
  • Reimagined the corporate website with a consistent design system;
  • Customized Wordpressbackend to update contents easily.








How We Got There


Understand Users

Develop Actionable Recommendations from Survey and Focus Group



During the discovery phase, we conducted focus group session and analyzed heatmaps and analytics to determine how to improve the old website for usability.













Better Navigation

Define the New Sitemap



We have gone through multiple workshops to define the new site structure. The goal is to help users find what they’re looking for, even when they’re not sure what it is yet.








Remake the Pages

Paper Sketches to Visualize New Page Structure



During the prototyping phase, I sketched out new ideas on paper to visualize the new page layout options.

The Clot Thickens - Infecting Health White Cells


Category: Student Project, 2D Games

Timeframe: 10/2015 - 12/2015

Team BacTEAMia: Angela Hamilton, Diptoman Mukherjee, Berenice Molina Ortega, Zeeshan Rasool, and Karen Bao

My Role: 2D Character Artist




The Clot Thickens is an arcade timing-based game about infecting healthy white cells, where the player has to precisely time jumps to survive. The project has been part of my Game Design course at the Centre for Digital Media.



Inspired by our project, our professor Dr. Dave Fracchia, who is also the Studio Head at the MGN Studio, purchased the concept IP and released the game the Clot Thickens - Level Designer.

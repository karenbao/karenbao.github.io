Shopify Design Challenge



iOS App / App Landing Page

Role: UX/UI Designer

From fitness to productivity to mental health, there’s a ton of apps to help us measure our lives, keep track of habits, and help us improve.

Imagine you’ll be launching a new app that helps someone understand how their daily choices help or hinder their goals every week. Design a single-page website to launch your life logging app to the world.

Insights

New technologies, like wearables and iPhone HealthKit, have been used to collect a large sum of health and fitness data for human beings. However, very few users are able to use their data wisely to achieve specific goals.

Concept

iLog is a goal-driven personal activity tracking and statistics management application for self-improvement. The application provides insights and recommendations of achievable goals to users based on their mobile devices’ and wearables’ data.
